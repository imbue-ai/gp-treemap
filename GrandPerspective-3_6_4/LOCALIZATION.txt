The latest version of GrandPerspective is available in the following
languages, besides English:

  * Finnish, by Mikko Oksalahti
  * French, by Marc Croteau
  * German, by Carsten Bracke, Klaus Rade, Helge Baumann and Michael
    Wagner
  * Greek, by Dimitris Pergelidis
  * Italian, by Mattia Mazzucchelli and Matteo Guarnerio
  * Portuguese, by Aristóteles Soares Benicio and Mauricio Panata
  * Simplified Chinese, by Albert Leng, Rex Chiu and Whyto
  * Spanish, by Jorge Ruiz Calpe
  * Turkish, by Alp Tunc and Emir Sarı
  * Dutch, by myself

Translations have also been provided for earlier versions of the
application. In particular, the following localizations have been
created:

  * Japanese, by Chris Li
  * Spanish, by Javier Alfonso
  * French, by Maxime Hadjinlian

Unfortunately, the application has evolved since then and these
localizations are not up to date anymore. Furthermore, Apple has
changed the mechanism used to internationalize applications.
GrandPerspective has been adapted to support this, which means that
additional effort is required to adapt these older localizations.

So I am looking for volunteers who can update the existing
localizations or who are willing to provide new ones. You can do so
for any language you speak fluently. You can post a message on the
GrandPerspective localization forum to express your interest:

  https://sourceforge.net/p/grandperspectiv/discussion/634668/

We can then take it from there.

Thanks again to everyone who has been providing localizations for
GrandPerspective so far.

Cheers,
